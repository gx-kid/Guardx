Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

01. Overview

Welcome to GuardX (Gx Kids Parent Control App). We prioritize the security of your family's data. This policy explains how our Parent Application handles information to ensure a safe monitoring environment.

02. Data Collection

Authentication

We use Google Sign-In for secure access. We only store your email address for account management.

Device Pairing

Encrypted pairing codes via Firebase are used to securely link the Parent and Child applications.

03. App Permissions

The GuardX Parent App requires specific permissions to function:

  • Notification Access: Used exclusively to deliver real-time parental alerts from the child's device.
  • Internet Access: Necessary for Firebase data synchronization and remote monitoring features.

04. Data Deletion

We believe in your right to be forgotten. You can request a total wipe of your data and account at any time.
